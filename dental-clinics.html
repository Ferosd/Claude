What we build

What we build for your practice

Not a generic booking widget. A system built around your practice management software, your appointment types, and the way your team already works.

24/7 Appointment Booking

An AI assistant on your website that answers patient questions, finds available slots, and books appointments around the clock. Integrates with Dentally, SOE, and Exact — patients book real slots, not placeholder requests.

No-Show Reduction System

Automated WhatsApp and SMS reminders at 72 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours before every appointment. Patients confirm, reschedule, or cancel with one tap. If they cancel, the next patient on the waitlist is offered the slot automatically.

Waitlist Automation

When a cancellation happens, the system instantly contacts the next patient on your waiting list. No receptionist time wasted on phone calls — the slot gets filled before you even know it was empty.

Patient Recall & Google Reviews

Automated recall reminders for 6-month check-ups, hygiene appointments, and treatment follow-ups. After every appointment, patients receive a friendly Google Review request. More reviews, better visibility, more new patients.
