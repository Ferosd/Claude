## 1. Contingency Fee

The Firm's fee will be calculated as a percentage of the **gross recovery** (settlement, judgment, or arbitration award), payable only if there is a recovery:

| Stage | Percentage |
|---|---|
| Settled before suit is filed | **33%** |
| Settled after suit is filed but before trial | **40%** |
| Tried to verdict or arbitrated to award | **45%** |

If there is no recovery, the Client owes no attorney fee. Costs and disbursements (see §3) remain the Client's responsibility regardless of outcome, unless waived in writing.

## 2. Costs and Disbursements

The Client is responsible for out-of-pocket costs incurred in pursuing the claim, including but not limited to:

- Court filing fees and service of process
- Medical record retrieval and copying charges
- Expert witness fees and depositions
- Travel and printing in excess of standard amounts

The Firm may advance such costs and recover them from any settlement or judgment **before** computing the contingency fee.

## 3. Insurance Coordination

The Firm will deal with **{{INSURANCE_PROVIDER}}** (policy **{{INSURANCE_POLICY}}**) and any third-party insurers on the Client's behalf. The Client agrees not to communicate directly with adjusters about the merits of the claim while represented.

## 4. Settlement Authority

No settlement will be accepted without the Client's written consent. The Firm will communicate every settlement offer received.

## 5. File Retention

The Firm will retain the case file for **seven (7) years** following final disposition. The Client may request a copy at any time.

## 6. Termination

Either party may terminate this engagement on written notice. The Firm reserves a lien for fees and costs based on the work performed up to the date of termination, calculated on a *quantum meruit* basis if termination occurs before recovery.
