What We Automate

Six automations that pay for themselves in the first month.

Lead Collection
Capture leads from any source — website, social, ads, events — and push them directly into your CRM with full context. Zero manual data entry.
Email Sequences
Personalized follow-up emails sent at exactly the right time based on lead behavior. First response in 30 seconds. Follow-ups every 2–3 days. Fully automated.
Meeting Booking
Prospects pick a time directly from your calendar link sent automatically after qualification. No back-and-forth. No scheduling overhead. Meetings appear instantly.
CRM Updates
Contact records updated automatically after every email opened, link clicked, or meeting attended. Your pipeline stays accurate without your team lifting a finger.

Internal Alerts
