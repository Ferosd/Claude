The Process

Live in 1–3 days after we receive your data

01

Data Collection

You share your documents, website content, FAQs, product catalogues, and policies. Any text-based resource your team uses to answer customer questions becomes your AI's knowledge base.

02

AI Training

We build and train your custom RAG model on your data. We configure its tone, personality, and escalation rules — so it answers exactly the way your best employee would.

03

Integration

We embed the chatbot on your website, app, or internal platform. We handle all technical configuration — you get a simple snippet of code to paste, or we do it for you.

04

Monitor & Improve

We track conversation logs, identify gaps, and retrain the model as your business evolves. You can flag any incorrect answer for immediate correction. It gets smarter over time.
