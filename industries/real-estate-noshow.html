How It Works

From first enquiry to “I’m ready” — every lead nurtured automatically

No more forgotten follow-ups. No more lost leads. Every buyer kept warm until they’re ready to move.

1

Lead enters CRM

From any source: website, Rightmove, Zoopla, social, phone. Automatically tagged and scored.

2

Warmth score assigned

AI scores each lead as hot, warm, or cold based on budget, timeline, and engagement.

3

Sequence begins

Hot: agent called in 5 min. Warm: Day 1/3/7/14/30 email + SMS. Cold: monthly market updates.

4

Behaviour tracked

Every open, click, and reply updates the lead score. Cold lead who clicks 3 listings? Now warm — agent notified.

5

Ready to buy?

When a lead reaches threshold score or requests a viewing, agent gets instant alert with full history and context.

Lead scoring runs continuously. A lead who goes quiet for 6 weeks then clicks three new listings is automatically upgraded and flagged to your team.
