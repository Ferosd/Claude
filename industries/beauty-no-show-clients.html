The Problem

Three empty chairs a day adds up to £30,000 a year in wasted revenue

Every no-show is more than a lost appointment. It’s wasted prep time, an unhappy therapist, and revenue that’s gone for good.

The Silence After Booking

A client books three weeks out. No reminder. No confirmation. Life happens. They forget — or they find somewhere closer. You only notice when the chair sits empty at 2 PM on a Thursday.

Cancellation Without Replacement

Someone cancels at 9 AM for a 10 AM slot. There’s no waitlist system. Your team calls around manually — by the time they reach someone available, the slot’s gone. An hour of revenue, lost.

The Emotional Toll

It’s not just money. You set up the room, mixed the colour, and waited. Nobody came. 40% of salon owners say no-shows harm their mental health — that frustration is real and it compounds.
